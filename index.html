ajax_pagination by ronalchn

Handles AJAX pagination in Rails 3.0+, loading content into an "AJAX section" when certain links are followed.

Demonstration

Below is a demonstration of some of the capabilities of AJAX Pagination. It shows a built-in animation on loading, fully working history and AJAX requests which can perform POST and DELETE requests, whether via a link or a form submission.
